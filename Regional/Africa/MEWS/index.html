Some comments

A number of potential features
  1. to get animations, enter a time range for the TIme:, e.g. Aug 2004 or Jun-Sep 2004.
  2. could change the zoom interface to appear more sophisticated (example).
Several Flaws in what we have so far
  1. rethink the spatial basis set: wouldn't we be better off with countries or states or districts instead of this somewhat arbitrary box?
  2. baseline comparisons: perhaps compare station data to what we are presenting.
  3. In general, the averaging time span and the area to average over should depend on both the use and the data.
The data are here. Note that there are more pixels in the data than on the image below: i.e. some pixels cannot be chosen at a resolution of 11km unless one zooms in first. That is the 11 km option only shows up on the time series page.
