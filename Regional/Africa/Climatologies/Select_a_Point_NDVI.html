Obtaining Climatologies

To obtain the local monthly climatology and standard deviation of NDVI simply move your cursor over any land area location on the map of Africa. and click.


You may ZOOM in on a desired region of the world by changing the latitude and longitude settings of the display. LATITUDE settings are located to the left of the world map, LONGITUDE settings are located below the map. To specify a new latitude range, edit the values in the boxes to the left of the map. Use "N" to indicate degrees north, "S" to indicate degrees south. Similarly, to specify new longitude settings, edit the values in the boxes below the plot. Use "W" to indicate degrees west and "E" to indicate degrees east.


The Climatology Plots

The base period for all of these plots is 1982 - 2004.
The data is in gridded format on a 0.1 by 0.1 degree grid.

The monthly climatology and standard deviation of NDVI for a selected point in Africa are displayed in two plots:

  • Long-term average monthly NDVI is displayed in the left plot. Horizontal lines are drawn at 0.1 intervals.
  • The Long-term standard deviation of monthly NDVI is plotted in the right plot. Horizontal lines are drawn at intervals of 0.5 standard deviation.

Select the Redraw button (to the lower-left of map) to hide HELP

Data: Monthly maximum USGS dekadal NDVIg data gridded to 0.1° x 0.1° lat/lon resolution
Base Period for Climatology:  1982-2004
Data Source:  NASA/USGS ADDS

Description:
The map on this page shows the monthly climatology of USGS NDVIg data using a base period of 1982-2004. Use the "Time" control above the map to see the climatology for other months of the year. To produce these maps, the maximum of the three dekadal NDVIg values per month are calculated at each point, then the long-term (1982-2004) average is calculated for each month. In the final step, the long-term average NDVIg values, originally in the Albers Equal Area Conic projection, are gridded onto a regular (geographic projection) 0.1° x 0.1° lat./lon. grid.

Click on any point on the continent to view bar graphs of the 1982-2004 monthly climatology and standard deviation of NDVI at that point.
