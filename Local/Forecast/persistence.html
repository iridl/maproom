Note
- Plots a), b) and c) show climate information for twelve months preceding the month of the current forecast.
- a) Precipitation anomaly: difference between observation and climatology (mean)
- b) Precipitation percentile with respect to the climatological distribution
- c) Lines indicate climatological 3-month lower tercile boundary (33 %ile, brown), the median (50 %ile, black) and upper tercile boundary (67 %ile, green) of the climatological (1979 - 1995) totals. The bars show the mean of the embedded 1-month precipitation climatology (e.g. when lines apply to Aug-Sep-Oct, the bar shows mean for September).
- d) shows the probability forecast in tercile categories for the 3-month period of the current forecast. Horizontal lines show thresholds for climatology (33%), enhanced (40%) and greatly enhanced (55%) probabilities.
