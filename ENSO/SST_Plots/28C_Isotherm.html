Monthly Sea Surface Temperature Greater Than 28 deg. C


Data:   Reynolds and Smith Version 2 monthly sea surface temperature (deg. C) on a 1.0 x 1.0 deg. lat/lon grid
Base Period for Climatology:  1971-2000
Data Source:  NCEP, Environmental Monitoring Center

Description:  Monthly sea surface temperatures above 28 deg. C are shaded. Thick black contour indicates climatological position of the 28 deg. C isotherm.

NCEP is an acronym for the US National Centers for Environmental Prediction.
