Change in Sea Surface Temperature Anomaly from Previous Month (deg. C)


Data:   Monthly sea surface temperature anomaly (deg. C) on a 1.0 x 1.0 deg. lat/lon grid
Base Period for Climatology:  1950-1979
Data Source:  NCEP, Environmental Monitoring Center

Description:  Change in sea surface temperature anomaly from the previous month contoured at 0.5 deg. C intervals.  Shading starts at +/- 0.5 deg. C. 

NCEP is an acronym for the US National Centers for Environmental Prediction.
