Weekly Sea Surface Temperature Anomaly Past 20 Weeks (deg. C)


Data:   Reynolds and Smith Version 2 weekly sea surface temperature anomaly (deg. C) on a 1.0 x 1.0 deg. lat/lon grid (1981-present)
Base Period for Climatology:  1971-2000 
Data Source:  NCEP, Environmental Monitoring Center

Description:  Sea surface temperature anomaly (deg. C) with contours drawn at 0.5 deg. C intervals.  Shading starts at +/- 0.5 deg. C.

NCEP is an acronym for the US National Centers for Environmental Prediction.
