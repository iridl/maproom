925 hPa Vector Wind Anomaly - Past 4 Months

Data:  Monthly 925 hPa wind components on a 2.5 x 2.5 deg. lat/lon grid
Base period for climatology:  1961-1990
Source:   NCEP, Reanalysis

Description:  Vector wind anomaly for the past 4 months.  Contours and shading indicate speed of anomalous wind in m/s.  Contour interval is 3 m/s.  NCEP is an acronym for the US National Centers for Environmental Prediction.
