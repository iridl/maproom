ENSO and All-India Rainfall



SST anomaly (bar color).  India rainfall (bar length)
The quintessential ENSO -- rainfall correlation is the connection between India rainfall and ENSO. The heights of the bars on the plot below give the relative strength of the rainfall in the India monsoon: up means greater than normal, down is a drought. The colors of the bars give the strength of ENSO for that year: red is a hot (El Niño) event, blue is a cold (La Niña) event, and off-white indicates near-normal temperatures. The plot indicate that most major droughts in India occur during El Niño events; they rarely happen during La Niña events.
Data Source: Indian Institute of Tropical Meteorology (IITM).
