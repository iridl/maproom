Data::   Monthly depth of the equatorial Pacific 20 deg. C isotherm (Jan 1980-present)
Base Period: 1980-present
Data Source:  NCEP, Environmental Monitoring Center, Climate Modeling Branch

Description:   The monthly depth of the equatorial Pacific 20 deg. C isotherm (a proxy for the location of the thermocline) is averaged from 175-100W. Anomalies in this depth are computed from a 1980-present base period are then standardized by dividing by the monthly standard deviation computed over the same base period. NCEP is an acronym for the US National Centers for Environmental Prediction.
