Climate and Meningitis in Africa

Epidemics of meningitis occur worldwide. The "meningitis belt" in the Sahel region of Africa, however, has the greatest incidence of the disease. Epidemics occur throughout the 'belt' in the dry season. They typically coincide with periods of very low humidity and dusty conditions and disappear with the onset of the rains, suggesting that these environmental factors may play an important role in the occurrence of the disease.
