Climate and Health Resource Room

Climate affects health in a number of ways. These effects may be direct, as with heat stress, or indirect, as with infectious diseases such as malaria and meningitis. This facility aims to explore and inform users about the climate-health relationship with an emphasis on the seasonal nature of that relationship, where appropriate. This "Climate and Health Resource Room" will begin with a focus on malaria and meningitis in Africa. It is our intention to increase the content of the resource room by including analyses that focus on other diseases and regions, as well those developed at other institutions.
