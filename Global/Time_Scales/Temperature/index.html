Maproom Checklist
Area-averaging increases signal while reducing noise. Selecting a single gridpoint may seem more precise, but results will often be less reliable.

Compare area-averaged results using nested regions of different sizes. If there is little change, higher confidence can be assigned to the smaller regions.
