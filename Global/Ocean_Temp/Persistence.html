Persistence of Monthly Sea Surface Temperature (SST) Anomaly
Number of past 3 months with sea surface temperature (SST) anomaly < -0.5 or > +0.5 deg. C.


Data:   Reynolds and Smith (OI v. 2) monthly sea surface temperature departure from the long-term average (deg. C) on a 1.0 x 1.0 deg. lat/lon grid
Base Period for Climatology:  1971-2000
Data Source:  NCEP, Environmental Monitoring Center

Description:  The plot indicates the number of the past 3 months (ending on the date shown on lower-left) which had monthly sea surface temperature anomalies greater than +0.5 deg. C or less than -0.5 deg. C. NCEP is an acronym for the (US) National Centers for Environmental Prediction.
