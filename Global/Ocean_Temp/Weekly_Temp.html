Weekly Sea Surface Temperature (deg. C)


Data:   Weekly sea surface temperature anomaly (deg. C) on a 1.0 x 1.0 deg. lat/lon grid (1981-present)
Base Period for Climatology:  1950-1979 
Data Source:  NCEP, Environmental Monitoring Center

Description:  Sea surface temperature (deg. C) with contours drawn at 4 deg. C intervals.

NCEP is an acronym for the US National Centers for Environmental Prediction.
