Weekly Sea Surface Temperature

This map displays weekly sea surface temperatures for the globe.

Weekly sea surface temperatures are from the Reynolds and Smith OISST version 2 sea surface temperature dataset. Shading indicates the weekly average sea surface temperature in °C for the week indicated. Contours are drawn at an interval of 2.0°C.
