Data: CAMS surface air temperature anomaly (deg. C) on a 2.0 x 2.0 deg. lat/lon grid
Base Period for Climatology:  1971-2000 over land, 1974-present over ocean areas
Data Source:  NCEP, Climate Prediction Center USA

Description: The surface air temperature anomalies shown are based on station observations over land and satellite data over oceans. Contours drawn at +/- 1 deg. C intervals. "CAMS" is an acronym for the "Climate Anomaly Monitoring System" in use at the Climate Prediction Center (CPC) at the US National Centers for Environmental Prediction (NCEP)
