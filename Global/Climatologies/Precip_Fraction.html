Data:  CAMS_OPI monthly climatological precipitation on a 2.5 x 2.5 deg. lat/lon grid
Base Period:  1961-1990

Description:  The plots indiacte the percentage of annual precipitation that occurs during each of the indicated seasons.  "Masked" areas in white indicate regions with less than 25 mm of precipitation during the 3-month season shown.  Regions shaded in green indicate areas that receive more than 30% of their annual precipitation, while areas shaded in brown
receive less than 20% of their annual rainfall during the indicated season.  Light blue indicates between 20-30%.  Contours drawn at intervals of 10%.
