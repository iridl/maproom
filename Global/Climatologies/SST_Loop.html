Monthly Climatological Sea Surface Temperature
Data:  Reynolds and Smith monthly sea surface temperature climatology on a 1.0 x 1.0 deg. lat/lon grid
Base Period for Climatology:  1950-1979
Data Source:  NCEP

Description:
Monthly sea surface temperature with contours drawn at 2 deg. C intervals.  NCEP is an acronym for the US National Centers for Environmental Prediction.
