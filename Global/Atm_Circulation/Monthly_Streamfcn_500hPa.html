Global Monthly Streamfunction Anomaly at 500 hPa

This map shows the monthly streamfunction climatology and anomalies at the 500 hPa pressure level in the atmosphere for the given month over the 1981-2010 base period.

Monthly streamfunction climatology values are shown as grey contours with a contour interval of 10E+06 m2/s; dashed contours represent negative values, and solid contours represent positive values. Positive streamfunction anomalies are shown in yellow and orange, and negative anomalies are shown in shades of blue and are also expressed in units of m2/s.

Dataset Documentation

Global Monthly Streamfunction Anomaly at 500 hPa
Data NCEP-NCAR Reanalysis monthly streamfunction at 500 hPa on a 2.5° lat/lon grid
Data Source NOAA National Centers for Environmental Prediction (NCEP)
Analysis Monthly streamfunction climatology and anomalies using the 1981-2010 base period.
