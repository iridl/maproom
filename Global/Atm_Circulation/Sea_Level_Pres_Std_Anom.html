Global Monthly Sea Level Pressure Standardized Anomaly

This is a contour map of monthly average sea level pressure standardized anomalies for the globe with respect to the 1981-2010 climatology.

The sea level pressure standardized anomalies are calculated from the NCEP-NCAR Reanalysis data set and mapped with a contour interval of 0.5 standard deviations.

Dataset Documentation

Global Monthly Sea Level Pressure Standardized Anomaly
Data NCEP-NCAR Reanalysis monthly sea level pressure on a 2.5° lat/lon grid
Data Source NOAA National Centers for Environmental Prediction (NCEP)
Analysis Contour map of monthly sea level pressure standardized anomalies with respect to the 1981-2010 base period
