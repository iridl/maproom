Seasonal Standardized Streamfunction Anomaly

This map shows the seasonal (3-month average) streamfunction climatology and standardized anomalies at the 250, 500, and 925 hPa pressure levels in the atmosphere using the 1981-2010 base period for the season shown.

Seasonal streamfunction climatology values are shown as grey contours with a contour interval of 10E+06 m2/s; dashed contours represent negative values and solid contours represent positive values. Positive standardized streamfunction anomalies are shown in yellow and orange, and negative anomalies are shown in shades of blue, with shading starting at +/-1 standard deviations and a contour interval of 0.5 standard deviations.

Dataset Documentation

Seasonal Standardized Streamfunction Anomaly
Data NCEP-NCAR Reanalysis monthly streamfunction at 250, 500, and 925 hPa on a 2.5° lat/lon grid
Data Source NOAA National Centers for Environmental Prediction (NCEP)
Analysis Seasonal (3-month average) streamfunction climatology and standardized anomalies using the 1981-2010 base period.
