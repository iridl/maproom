Information on weather, climate, and agricultural developments worldwide, along with detailed charts and tables of agrometeorological information that are appropriate for the season, from U.S. Department of Commerce, NOAA, and the U.S. Department of Agriculture

North American Drought Monitor (NCDC)

The North America Drought Monitor (NADM) is a cooperative effort between drought experts in Canada, Mexico and the United States to monitor drought across the continent on an ongoing basis.

U.S. Drought Monitor (NDMC)

The U.S. Drought Monitor is produced in partnership between the National Drought Mitigation Center at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln, the United States Department of Agriculture, and the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration.
