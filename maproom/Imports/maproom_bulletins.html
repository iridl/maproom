Preliminary Climate Diagnostics Bulletin (CPC)

Updates from the first week of each month for the CPC Climate Diagnostics Bulletin

ENSO Quick Look (IRI)

Monthly discussion of the current state of the El Niño-Southern Oscillation (ENSO) from the IRI.

South America Climate Analyses (CPTEC)

Monthly and daily precipitation and temperature analyses for South America. Courtesy of Centro de Previsão de Tempo e Estudos Climáticos (CPTEC).

Emerging El Nino Conditions: Notes for the Global Health Community (IRI)

An quasi-annual discussion to inform health planners and practitioners on methods to monitor the vulnerability of communities and provide time sensitive information for interventions to reduce negative health impacts.

ENSO Diagnostic Discussion (CPC)

A monthly analysis and summary of current ENSO conditions, from the Climate Prediction Center (CPC)

Weekly Tropical Climate Note (BOM)

A weekly discussion of the state of the climate in the tropics, from the Australian Bureau of Meteorology (BOM).

El Niño Wrap-Up (BOM)

A regular commentary on the El Niño-Southern Oscillation, from the Australian Bureau of Meteorology (BOM).

TAO/TRITON Data Display

Real-time data from moored ocean buoys for improved detection, understanding and prediction of El Niño and La Niña. TAO is a project of the Pacific Marine Environmental Laboratory (PMEL) of NOAA.

NASA Worldview

Satellite Imagery for Vegetation, Dust Storms, Fires, Drought, Smoke Plumes, Ash Plumes, Air Quality, Severe Storms, and Floods
