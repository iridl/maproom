Monthly Sea Level Pressure

This is a contour map of monthly average sea level pressure for the globe.

The sea level pressure values are from the NCEP-NCAR Reanalysis data set and are mapped here in units of millibars (mb), or hectopascals (hPa), with a contour interval of 2 mb.
