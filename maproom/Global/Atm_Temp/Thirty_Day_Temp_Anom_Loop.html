Animation of Thirty-Day Running Average 925 hPa Temperature Anomaly

This map shows a 90-day animation of the thirty-day running average 925 hPa temperature anomaly for the globe.

Thirty-day running average 925 hPa air temperature anomalies are shown in units of °C. Red colors indicate temperatures above the 1981-2010 climatological average, and shades of blue indicate below-normal temperatures.
