Animation of Thirty-Day Running Average 925 hPa Standardized Temperature Anomaly

This map shows a 90-day animation of the thirty-day running average 925 hPa standardized temperature anomalies for the globe.

Shading for the standardized anomalies starts at +/- 1 s.d. Yellow and orange colors indicate temperatures above the 1981-2010 climatological average, and shades of blue indicate below-normal temperatures.
