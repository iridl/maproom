Monthly Climatological Surface Air Temperature

This map shows an animation of monthly climatological surface air temperature (in °C) through the course of the year using the 1971-2000 base period.

Shading indicates the monthly climatological surface air temperature in units of °C. Contours are drawn at an interval of 5°C.

Dataset Documentation

Monthly Climatological Surface Air Temperature

Data
TS2.1 Global Monthly Time Series of monthly mean surface air temperature on a 0.5° lat/lon grid using the 1971-2000 base period
Data Source
University of East Anglia (UEA) Climatic Research Unit (CRU) (CRU TS2.1)
Analysis
Monthly climatology of monthly mean surface air temperature using the 1971-2000 base period
