Climatologies

These climatologies give the monthly (or seasonal) behavior in an average year for temperature, precipitation, and wind.

Monthly Climatological Precipitation Frequency
This map shows an animation of monthly climatological precipitation frequency -- the average number of days per month with measurable precipitation -- for the 1971-2000 base period.
Monthly Change in Climatological Precipitation
This map shows an animation of the month-to-month change in monthly climatological precipitation (in mm/month) through the course of the year based on CAMS-OPI data using the 1981-2010 base period.
Seasonal Fraction of Annual Precipitation
This map shows the percentage of annual precipitation that occurs during the indicated 3-month season, based upon climatological precipitation using the 1981-2010 base period from the monthly CPC Merged Analysis of Precipitation (CMAP) dataset.
Monthly Climatological Precipitation
This map shows an animation of monthly climatological precipitation (in mm/month) through the course of the year based on CAMS-OPI data using the 1981-2010 base period.
Monthly Climatological Sea Surface Temperature
This map shows an animation of monthly climatological sea surface temperature (in °C) through the course of the year based on Reynolds and Smith OISST Version 2 data using the 1971-2000 base period.
Select a Point Climatology
Click anywhere over land on the map to see barplots of the local monthly climatology of precipitation, monthly average of daily maximum, minimum, and mean temperature, wet-day frequency, and ground frost frequency.

This map shows an animation of monthly climatological surface air temperature (in °C) through the course of the year using the 1971-2000 base period.
