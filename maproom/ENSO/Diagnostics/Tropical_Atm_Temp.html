Column-Average Tropospheric Temperature Anomaly over the Tropics

This is a map of monthly tropospheric temperature anomaly in the global tropics, averaged from 850 hPa to 200 hPa.

Yellow to red colors represent positive temperature anomalies, and blue colors represent negative temperature anomalies. The climatological base period used to calculate the anomalies is 1981-2010. Temperature anomalies are contoured at an interval of 0.5°C

Dataset Documentation

Atmospheric Temperature Data

Data
NCEP-NCAR Reanalysis monthly air temperature from 850 hPa to 200 hPa (in °C) on a 2.5° lat/lon grid
Data Source
NOAA National Centers for Environmental Prediction (NCEP) (NCEP/NCAR Reanalysis)
Analysis
Map of monthly air temperature anomalies (1981-2010 base period) in the global tropics, averaged from 850 hPa to 200 hPa.
