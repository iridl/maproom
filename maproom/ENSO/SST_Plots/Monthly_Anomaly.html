Monthly Sea Surface Temperature Anomaly

This map displays monthly sea surface temperature anomalies for the Pacific Ocean.

Monthly sea surface temperature anomalies are calculated with respect to the 1971-2000 climatology. Yellow to red colors on the map indicate areas where sea surface temperatures for the month shown are above the climatological value for that month of the year, and blue shades indicate where sea surface temperatures are below normal.
