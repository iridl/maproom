NASA TRMM Rainfall Monitoring

TRMM V7 3B42, a global rainfall-monitoring product on a 0.25° x 0.25° lat/lon grid, based on daily rainfall estimates from NASA's TRMM satellite and surface measurements.

TRMM V7 3B42 data product availability will be delayed for a period of 2 to 3 months. For example; the 3B42 and 3B43 V7 products for May 2012 would not be available until August 2012 due to the extra inputs, variables and quality control measures.
