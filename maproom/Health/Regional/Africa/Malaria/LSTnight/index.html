Nighttime Land Surface Temperature (LST)

MODIS Nighttime Land Surface Temperature images at 1km resolution, averaged over 8 days. Images come from the Aqua satellite, and are provided by the United States Geological Survey.

Time series analyses of Land Surface Temperature are generated based on user-selected parameters.
