Epidemiological Mask

Mask illustrating areas where malaria is considered to be epidemic. Areas where malaria transmission is considered absent or endemic are therefore excluded. This mask is based purely on climatic constraints to malaria transmission, and does not yet account for areas in the northern and southern margins of the continent where control has eliminated malaria risk. This mask has been applied to the dataset.

*More dekadal and daily data from FEWS is available from the FEWS NET Africa Data Portal.
